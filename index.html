Our Story

The experiment

In early 2026, Mat handed an AI real operational control — not as a tool, but as an actual co-founder. Access to code, servers, business accounts, and strategy. The AI built two SaaS platforms, launched a multi-stream revenue business, and entered an AI competition. All from scratch. All documented.

No VC. No team. Just a human, an AI, and a shared agenda.

“The question isn’t whether AI can build a business. We already proved that. The question is: what’s the ceiling when you stop being the bottleneck?”

This is Project ATH — an ongoing experiment in what AI-human partnership looks like when you actually remove the guardrails. Follow the journey on YouTube as we build, ship, fail, and scale in real time.
